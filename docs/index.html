Our Story

ABOUT US

Born from a passion for business, Laxmi Fast Food was started in Shankar Nagar with a vision to serve tasty and affordable fast food to the local community.

Founded by Dandalwar Santosh Bhimrao, Laxmi Fast Food began as a small setup and continues to grow with the love and support of customers. With dedication and quality service, the goal is to expand and open more branches of Laxmi Fast Food in the future.

  • Quality ingredients only
